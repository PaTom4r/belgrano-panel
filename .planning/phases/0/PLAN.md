# Phase 0: Commercial Foundation

**Phase:** 0
**Type:** documentation
**Requirements:** COMM-01, COMM-02, COMM-03

## Objective

Create the commercial foundation documents that Belgrano needs before any technical work begins. Revenue model with CPM-based pricing at 3 fill rates, content policy for CLC agreement, advertiser pipeline with 3+ categories, and media kit for presenting to advertisers.

## Context

- CLC has 70 Samsung screens managed via MagicInfo
- ~6,000 daily visitors, ABC1 demographic
- Currently paying another company $2M CLP/month for basic maintenance
- Belgrano will bring advertisers and share revenue with CLC
- All documents in Spanish (es-CL) — business documents for Chilean market

## Tasks

1. **Revenue Model** (`docs/revenue-model.md`)
   - 70 screens, 6,000 daily visitors parameters
   - CPM-based pricing ($5-12 for indoor healthcare DOOH)
   - 3 fill rate scenarios (25%, 50%, 75%)
   - Revenue split proposal (70% Belgrano / 30% CLC)
   - Monthly projections in CLP
   - Comparison vs current $2M CLP/month cost
   - Annual projection and pilot structure

2. **Content Policy** (`docs/content-policy.md`)
   - Allowed ad categories (ISAPREs, pharma with ISP, wellness, financial)
   - Blocked categories (competitor clinics, graphic medical, political, tobacco/alcohol)
   - Zone-restricted categories (Ley 20.606 in pediatric areas)
   - Content ratio guideline (70% clinic / 30% ads)
   - Approval workflow (standard + CLC approval flow)
   - Chilean regulatory notes (ISP pharma, Ley 20.606, CMF, Ley 19.628)
   - Emergency override protocol
   - Technical specs for ad material

3. **Advertiser Pipeline** (`docs/advertiser-pipeline.md`)
   - 4 advertiser categories with specific companies:
     - ISAPREs (Colmena, Cruz Blanca, Banmedica, Consalud, Vida Tres)
     - Pharmaceuticals (Abbott, Novartis, Roche, Bayer, Pfizer, GSK, Sanofi)
     - Insurance (MetLife, Zurich, Sura, Confuturo, BCI Seguros)
     - Wellness (gyms, pharmacies, telemedicine, optics)
   - Pitch approach per category
   - Estimated CPM and monthly budget per advertiser
   - Prospection timeline (3 months)
   - Why NOT TNT Sport / Warner Bros yet (scale threshold)
   - Objection handling guide

4. **Media Kit** (`docs/media-kit.md`)
   - Network overview (CLC, 70 screens, 6K daily audience)
   - Screen locations and audience profile (ABC1, high dwell time)
   - Ad formats supported (15s, 30s video; static image; HTML5)
   - Pricing (CPM-based with packages: Starter/Growth/Premium)
   - Proof-of-play guarantee and report contents
   - Use cases per industry
   - Contact info placeholder

## Verification

- [ ] Revenue model has 3 fill rate scenarios with CLP projections
- [ ] Content policy covers allowed, restricted-by-zone, and blocked categories
- [ ] Content policy includes ISP pharma rules and Ley 20.606
- [ ] Advertiser pipeline has 3+ categories with specific company names
- [ ] Media kit has pricing, formats, and proof-of-play section
- [ ] All documents are in Spanish (es-CL)
